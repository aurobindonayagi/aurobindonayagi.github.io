SEO & Content Strategy
  • Target keywords: 'terracotta roof tiles India', 'clay floor tiles Chennai', 'jaali wall design'
  • On-page: meta titles, structured data (Product schema), image alt-text, mobile-first page speed
  • Content: 2 blog posts/month, project showcases, comparison guides, FAQ pages per product
  • Off-page: location pages for Bangalore & Chennai, guest posts on architecture blogs
  • Goal: 50+ keywords in Top 10 · DA from ~15 → 30+
📱
Social Media Strategy
  • Instagram — 1 post/day: aesthetic shots, reels, before/after reveals
  • Pinterest — 10 pins/week: product + project boards linked to product pages
  • LinkedIn — 3 posts/week: project case studies, sustainability, thought leadership
  • YouTube — 2 videos/month: factory tours, installation guides, testimonials
  • Content pillars: Inspire · Educate · Showcase · Engage
📢
Google Ads Strategy
  • Search Ads (50% — ₹25K): high-intent keywords, exact & phrase match
  • Display/Remarketing (20% — ₹10K): product imagery to past visitors
  • Performance Max (20% — ₹10K): AI-optimized across Search, Display, YouTube, Gmail
  • Local Campaigns (10% — ₹5K): Bangalore/Chennai footfall + WhatsApp enquiries
  • Target by Month 6: 80–100 leads/mo · CTR >3% · CPL <₹500
📘
Meta Ads Strategy
  • TOFU (₹10K) — Reach & Brand Awareness: video reels + carousel to architecture/home renovation audience
  • MOFU (₹15K) — Traffic & Lead Gen: lookalike audiences, lead form ads + catalogue ads
  • BOFU (₹10K) — Conversion: remarketing to video viewers, click-to-WhatsApp ads
  • Pro Tip: UGC-style videos & architect testimonials for highest CTR in this category
