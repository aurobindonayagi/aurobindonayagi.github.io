Grindhaus — 360° Digital Marketing Audit

A comprehensive real-brand digital marketing audit for Grindhaus by Galactic Gourmet Grub LLP (mygrindhaus.com) — covering SEO, content gaps, social media, email, Amazon, and a 90-day action plan.

✓ Real Brand · Live Audit · March 2026

Grindhaus — Stone-Ground Sauces & Nut Butters

Preservative-free, vegan, gluten-free, FSSAI certified. Ships pan-India via Shopify. Founded by Prashanth Chander (ex Four Seasons, Leela, Hard Rock Cafe). Products: Hot Madras Mix, Madras Pepper Garlic Curry, Kaara Onion Relish, Madras Heat (Gunpowder Peanut Butter), Thecha Blaze, Hazellux 55 Dark, Pineapple & Ginger Marmalade.

Brand Strengths

✓ Strong differentiated brand story — stone-ground, slow process, no preservatives
✓ Founder has 15+ years F&B and luxury hospitality experience
✓ Over 1,000 jars sold — early traction proven
✓ 4.8/5 star rating across 34 reviews — high customer satisfaction
✓ FSSAI licensed, ships next day from Chennai
✓ Unique positioning: Indian flavours in global everyday formats

Critical Issues Identified

🔍

SEO — Accordion Recipe Trap

10+ recipes hidden in dropdown format — Google cannot index collapsed content. Each recipe needs its own URL, title tag, and meta description to rank.

📄

Missing Product Schema

No structured data on product pages — star ratings don't appear in Google search results. Meta descriptions are auto-generated and not keyword-optimised.

📧

Weak Email Capture

Newsletter CTA has no incentive. No welcome sequence, no abandoned cart recovery, and no post-purchase email sequence.

📱

Instagram Needs Reels

Likely over-reliant on static product photos. Recipe carousels and food demo Reels would drive website traffic, saves, and shares.

🛒

Amazon — A+ Content Missing

Listings need keyword-rich titles and benefit-led bullet points. Short product videos can increase conversion by up to 80% in food category.

🔗

No Internal Linking

Recipe pages don't link to product pages — missed conversion opportunity for someone reading a Kara Kozhambu recipe.

Top Keyword Opportunities

kara kozhambu recipeSouth Indian curry paste buy onlineclean label food Indiahealthy nutella alternative Indiagunpowder peanut butterSouth Indian sauce for dosapreservative free condiments India

90-Day Action Plan

Week 1–2 · Immediate Fixes
  • Convert accordion recipes into indexed blog posts
  • Fix meta titles & descriptions with target keywords
  • Add Product schema for star ratings in search
  • Add image alt-text to all product images
  • Remove Sold Out item from homepage
Month 1 · Content Launch
  • 2 SEO blog posts per week targeting recipe keywords
  • Internal links from all recipes → product pages
  • Upgrade email CTA: '3 free recipes + 10% off'
  • Set up post-purchase email sequence
  • Launch #mygrindhaus branded hashtag campaign
Month 2–3 · Growth & Social
  • Launch Reels content strategy — recipe demos
  • Outreach to 10 micro-influencers (South Indian food niche)
  • Optimise Amazon listings with A+ Content
  • Founder story & stone-grinding content series
  • Set up GA4 conversion tracking
Month 3–6 · Scale & Targets
  • 50+ keywords in Top 20 Google results
  • 1,000+ email subscribers via lead magnets
  • 5,000+ Instagram followers
  • Pinterest as food discovery channel
  • YouTube 'Cook with Grindhaus' series
